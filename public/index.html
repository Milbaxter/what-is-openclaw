ü§ñ What is OpenClaw, really?

OpenClaw is a free, open-source personal AI assistant that runs on your own computer. Think of it like having your own Siri or Alexa — but one you fully control.

üí¨ Which apps does it work with?

WhatsApp, Telegram, iMessage, Slack, Discord, Microsoft Teams, Signal, Google Chat, and more. It meets you where you already are.

üîí Is OpenClaw safe?

It's open-source and runs on your machine, which is great for privacy. But it requires technical knowledge to use safely, and some third-party add-ons have had security issues.

üÜö OpenClaw vs ChatGPT vs Siri

The big difference: you own and control it. Your data stays with you, not on someone else's servers. It's free, open-source, and works across all your messaging apps.

üìà Why is everyone talking about it?

It went from 9,000 to 100,000+ GitHub stars in days. Created by Peter Steinberger as a weekend project, it became one of the fastest-growing open-source projects ever.

üß© What are OpenClaw skills?

Skills are add-ons — like apps for your phone, but for your AI assistant. They can add web browsing, calendar management, smart home control, and much more.

A few days later, the creator Peter Steinberger changed it again to "OpenClaw" because "Moltbot never quite rolled off the tongue."
